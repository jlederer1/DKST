DKST Package Documentation

The DKST (Deep Knowledge Structure Theory) package offers a suite of tools for generating, sampling, manipulating, and visualizing knowledge structures, including quasi-orders and surmise relations. It combines elements of Knowledge Structure Theory (KST) and Deep Learning (DL) approaches for data-driven domain modelling.
